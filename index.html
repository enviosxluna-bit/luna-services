Who We Are

About ANS Wireless

ANS Wireless is a licensed money services business (MSB) operating as a DBA of Millennials Wireless, based in Oxnard, California. We specialize in international money transfers and remittances, serving the Oxnard community with a commitment to reliability, transparency, and personal service.

With two convenient locations and partnerships with the leading remittance networks — including Viamericas, MoneyGram, Vigo, Orlandi Valuta, and Intermex — we offer our customers a wide selection of transfer options and competitive exchange rates.

We are fully licensed and compliant with all federal and state regulations, including Bank Secrecy Act (BSA) and Anti-Money Laundering (AML) requirements, ensuring every transaction is safe and secure.
