Who We Are

About Luna Services

Luna Services is a licensed money services business (MSB) based in Oxnard, California, operating as a DBA of Millennials Wireless. We specialize in international money transfers and remittances, serving the Oxnard community with a commitment to reliability, transparency, and personal service.

With two convenient locations and partnerships with the leading remittance networks — including Viamericas, MoneyGram, Vigo, Orlandi Valuta, Intermex, Boss Rev, Intercambio, and Golden Money — we offer our customers the widest selection of transfer options and competitive exchange rates.

We are fully licensed and compliant with all federal and state regulations, including Bank Secrecy Act (BSA) / Anti-Money Laundering (AML) requirements, ensuring that every transaction is safe and secure.
