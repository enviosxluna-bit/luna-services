If you opt in to receive SMS messages from ANS Wireless:

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in these limited circumstances:

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include physical security at our locations, access controls, and encryption where applicable.

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, or as required by applicable law. Money transfer records are typically retained for a minimum of five (5) years as required by federal BSA/AML regulations.
