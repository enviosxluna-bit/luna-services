1. Program Description

ANS Wireless operates an SMS messaging program to communicate with customers who have opted in to receive text messages. Messages may include:

  • Daily or weekly exchange rate updates for money transfers to Mexico and Latin America

  • Important service updates (location hours, new services, etc.)

  • Automated exchange rate responses when customers text DOLAR

3. How to Opt Out

You can cancel your SMS subscription at any time. To opt out:

After you send STOP, we will send you one final confirmation message. You will not receive any additional marketing messages. You may opt back in at any time by texting LUNA to (805) 303-3313.
